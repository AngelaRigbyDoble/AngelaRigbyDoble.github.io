About me

I always loved art. For many years my working life led me in other directions, but in the end I got back to it. I lived in London at the time, and exhibited in several galleries, including a couple of one-woman shows.

Now in North Wales, I sell in local galleries and exhibitions and have won a number of prizes and awards.

Most of the pictures here are for sale as originals, others as I am available for commissions, and will consider a range of subjects - please enquire.
